The no pressure approach with nice golf carts they loan you to see the wide selection of about 400 new units that are all open for review. Even though this is not our first RV, the time taken by Bill, Steve and the hospitality of the entire staff allowed us to fully understand all aspects of the new motor home. We are on the road now with absolutely fond memories of the MHSRV experience. Well worth the trip to Texas for sure...
