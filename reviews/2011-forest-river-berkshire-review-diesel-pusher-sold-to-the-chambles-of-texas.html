2011 Forest River Berkshire Review, Diesel Pusher sold to Chambles of Texas

★★★★★
Diesel Pusher · Delivered to Texas
 Mr. O'Banion, I just want to say thanks for employing a great Professional Staff at your RV sales and service facility. I recently purchased a 2011 Forest River Berkshire. The knowledgeable Sales Associate was Jimmy Campana. Jimmy was friendly and ready to answer any questions I had about different motorhome models. I originally thought I would purchase a gas powered motorhome, after discussions with Jimmy I chose the diesel pusher. The most impressive thing about Jimmy was his no pressure sale. I have visited several other motorhome sales companies and they pressure the purchaser to buy the coach of their choice and to make a quick decision on the purchase. The walk through with Bill Heath was also pleasant. Bill was knowledgeable about the coach I purchased and did not rush through the walk through. At the end he commented they if I had any additional question please call and at that time he gave me his business card. I must admit when we first came to your business I was some what over whelmed by it's size and expected and arrogant sales staff. I was wrong! Thanks again for building a great team and fostering a pleasant sales environment.

— Chambles, Texas
