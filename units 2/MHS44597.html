About This RV

The 2026 Dynamax Isata 3 Series model 24TW is approximately 24 feet 7 inches in length powered by a I4 Turbo 208HP diesel engine on a Mercedes-Benz sprinter chassis. Additional features include the quad-view camera system, magnetic cab sun shades, rear Bilstein gas-charged shock absorbers, aluminum truss-structured roof with fiberglass skin, Sikkens full body paint with premium cut & buffed finish, front-end Diamond Shield paint protection, dual-pane acrylic windows with integrated screens/night shades, solid surface kitchen countertops with edge lighting, USB/USB-C Charging Port in Living Area, 4x4 HDMI matrix, Truma Aqua-Go comfort plus On-demand hot water heater, MaxxAir Fans with Built-In rain covers in bathroom, 2,000W Inverter and much more.
